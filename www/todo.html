The dred repository

The dred repository is simply a central store of user-created content including extensions and plugins, themes and configs. It's free to use and supports both commercial and non-commercial content.

By having everything stored in a central repository, users can have easy access to a wealth of content while extension developers can have access to better exposure and commercial opportunities.

Commercial and non-commercial derivatives

The dred platform includes libdred which can be used to develop both commercial and non-commercial derivatives of the core dred program. The advantage of doing things this way you can save on development costs while gaining access to a professional quality toolkit.

For non-commercial projects you can use dred as the foundations for free and when you go commercial you just pay us an industry standard cut in return.

Professional tools for developers

The dred project began as a result of frustration due to the lack of professional quality, lightweight development tools which worked consistently between the major platforms. The dred project is putting an end to this problem.
